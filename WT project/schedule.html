SCHEDULE/SPLIT

Now that youve seen the best effective exercises for the various muscle groups you might be wondering how to go about your workouts
Whether you are a very busy businessman coming from THE WALL STREET or an 18year old left with lots of time we have a plan for every person.
With that said it is very important to know consistency is very important for your efforts to yield results
The minimum number of days at the gym required by any person is 3.Thus we have workouts structured with resistance training for 3day split,4 day split,5day split,6 day split.

  1. 3 DAY SPLIT

      DAY 1:FULL BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 2:FULL BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 3:FULL BODY WORKOUT

    Pick any two variations for the larger muscle groups like Chest,Back,Legs and One variation for smaller muscle groups like the biceps,triceps and shoulders.Perform 3 sets of each variation for 12-15repititions.

  2. 4 DAY SPLIT

      DAY 1:UPPER BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 2:LOWER BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 3:UPPER BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 4:LOWER BODY WORKOUT

    For the upper body session pick 3 variations from the 5 for the Chest ,Back,Triceps groups and 2 variations for the biceps and shoulders.Do three sets of 12-15 repititions
    For the lower body session pick any 4 variations.Perform 4-5 sets of 12-15 repititions

  3. 5 DAY SPLIT

      DAY 1:UPPER BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 2:LOWER BODY WORKOUT
      DAY 3:PUSH
      DAY 4:PULL
      DAY 5:LEGS

    Ur workout will be same as that of the 4 day split for upper and lower body workout days.
    On the push day youll have to engage your chest,triceps and shoulders.Pick any 3 variations for each group and perform 3 sets of 15-20 repititions with lighter weights.
    On your pull day you engage your biceps,back and rear delts.Pick 3 variations for biceps,back and 2 for delts and perform 15-20 repititions for 3 sets.
    On your leg day perform 5 sets of all the 5 variations for 15 repititions with light weights.

  4. 6 DAY SPLIT

      DAY 1:PUSH
      DAY 2:PULL
      DAY 3:LEGS
      DAY 4:PULL
      DAY 5:PUSH
      DAY 6:LEGS

    On the push day youll have to engage your chest,triceps and shoulders.Pick any 3 variations for each group and perform 3 sets of 12-15 repititions .
    On your pull day you engage your biceps,back and rear delts.Pick 3 variations for biceps,back and 2 for delts and perform 12-15 repititions for 3 sets.
    On your leg day perform 5 sets of all the 5 variations for 15 repititions with light weights.

  5. CARDIO DAYS

    It is highly recommended that you perform cardio on at least two days.Depending on your schedule and your fitness level you can structure it any way you want
    There are three types of cardio you can perform.

      LISS-Low Intensity Steady State Cardio:

      walking or elliptical or cycling for 1 hour straight.

      MISS-Medium Intensity Steady State Cardio:

      Medium paced that takes your heartbeat from 72 per minute to around 80-85 beats per minute.

      HISS-High Intensity Steady State Cardio:

      High paced sprinting or cycling for as fast as you can for 30 seconds and resting for a minute and again following the same set taking your heartbeat above 120 beats per minute

    For those that are overweight by 50-100lbs,I would recommend you to do LISS FOR ABOUT AN HOUR everyday.
    For those that are moderately overweight do MISS for 45minutes every alternate day
    To those who want to lose only those few pounds of fat HISS for 20 min is recommended every alternate day.
